Sharing Food Is Simpler Than You Think

Three easy steps to turn your surplus into someone's next meal.

1

Snap & Post

Take a quick photo of the food you want to share, add a short description, and set your pickup window. It takes less than a minute.

2

Neighbor Claims

A nearby community member sees your listing, claims the item, and arranges a pickup time that works for both of you.

3

Share & Smile

Hand off the food at your doorstep. You've just prevented waste, fed a neighbor, and strengthened your community.
