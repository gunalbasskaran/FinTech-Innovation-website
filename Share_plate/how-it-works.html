Sharing Food Is Simpler
Than You Think

Whether you have leftovers from last night or a bumper crop from your garden, SharePlate makes it easy to share with neighbors who can use it.

Three Steps to Zero Waste

From surplus to shared in under two minutes.

1

Snap & Post

Open SharePlate, take a photo of your surplus food, add a brief description including dietary info, and set a pickup window. Your listing goes live to nearby neighbors instantly.

2

Neighbor Claims

A community member within your radius spots your listing, taps "Claim," and sends you a short message to confirm pickup. You stay in control of who, when, and where.

3

Share & Smile

Hand off the food at your doorstep, porch, or a designated drop-off point. Both of you earn a community trust rating, and the food saved is tracked in your impact dashboard.

Built for Businesses Too

Restaurants, bakeries, grocery stores, and cafés waste billions of dollars in unsold food every year. SharePlate gives your business a smarter alternative to the dumpster.

  • List unsold items in seconds with bulk-posting tools
  • Receive a verified monthly impact report for tax deductions
  • Earn a "Community Partner" badge displayed to local users
  • Schedule recurring end-of-day pickups automatically
  • Boost your visibility with eco-conscious local customers

Your Safety Comes First

We maintain clear guidelines so every exchange is safe, respectful, and reliable.

Verified Community

Every member completes email verification and agrees to our community code of conduct before sharing or claiming food.

Food Safety Standards

Clear listing requirements for preparation dates, ingredient details, allergen labels, and storage conditions keep things transparent.

Trust Ratings

After each exchange, both parties rate the experience. Consistent low ratings result in account review, keeping the community honest.

Report & Support

If anything feels off, our one-tap reporting system and dedicated support team are available around the clock to help resolve issues.
