Our Story

Building the Future of Money

We started NovaPay with a single belief: moving money should be as instant and free as sending a message. Today, we are turning that vision into reality for millions worldwide.

Our Mission

Democratize Access to Global Finance

We exist to eliminate the barriers that prevent people from fully participating in the global economy. Whether you are a freelancer in Lagos, a startup in São Paulo, or a family in rural Indonesia — you deserve the same financial tools as someone on Wall Street. NovaPay is building the infrastructure to make that possible.

Our Vision

A World Where Money Moves Freely

We envision a financial system that is open, instant, and universally accessible. No borders, no gatekeepers, no excessive fees. By 2030, we aim to serve over 500 million users and process $1 trillion in annual transaction volume — making financial inclusion not just an aspiration, but a reality.

What Drives Us

Our Core Principles

Every decision at NovaPay is guided by a simple framework: does this make financial services faster, cheaper, or more accessible? If the answer isn't yes to at least one, we don't ship it.

✓ Transparency First — No hidden fees, no fine print, no surprises. Every cost is shown upfront before you confirm.
✓ Security by Design — End-to-end encryption, zero-knowledge architecture, and SOC 2 Type II compliance protect every transaction.
✓ Inclusivity — Our platform supports 30+ languages and works on low-bandwidth connections to reach underserved communities.
✓ Speed as Standard — Sub-second settlement is not a premium feature. It's the default for every NovaPay user.

Milestones

Our Journey

From a whiteboard sketch to a global platform — every milestone has brought us closer to our mission.

Q1 2023

The Idea Is Born

Alex and Sarah meet at a fintech conference in Singapore. They sketch the first NovaPay architecture on a napkin over coffee.

Q3 2023

Seed Funding Secured

Raised $8.5M in seed funding from Sequoia Capital and Andreessen Horowitz to build the initial platform and settlement network.

Q1 2024

Beta Launch

Launched private beta in 12 countries with 50,000 early adopters. Processed $100M in transactions within the first 90 days.

Q3 2024

Series A — $42M

Closed Series A led by Tiger Global. Expanded the team to 120 employees across offices in San Francisco, London, and Singapore.

Q1 2025

Global Expansion

Launched in 190+ countries. Crossed 1 million active users and surpassed $5B in cumulative transaction volume.

2026

What's Next

Launching NovaPay Business, our enterprise payment suite. Building DeFi bridges for seamless crypto-to-fiat conversion.
