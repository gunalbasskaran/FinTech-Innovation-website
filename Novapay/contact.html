FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to the questions we hear most often. Can't find what you're looking for? Send us a message above.

NovaPay is currently available in 190+ countries and territories. We support money transfers, currency conversion, and virtual card issuance in all these regions. Physical card delivery is available in 45 countries, with more being added every month. Check our coverage map on the app for the most up-to-date information.
NovaPay charges a flat 0.1% fee on international transfers — that is 10 cents on every $100 sent. There are no hidden currency conversion markups. We use the mid-market exchange rate (the same rate you see on Google) with zero markup. For Starter plan users, the first $5,000/month in transfers is completely free.
NovaPay-to-NovaPay transfers are instant — typically under 50 milliseconds regardless of where the sender and recipient are located. Transfers to external bank accounts depend on the receiving bank's processing speed, but most arrive within 1–2 hours. In supported countries with instant payment rails, external transfers also arrive within seconds.

We maintain SOC 2 Type II certification, PCI DSS Level 1 compliance, and employ 256-bit AES encryption for all data at rest and in transit. Additionally, our zero-knowledge architecture means even NovaPay employees cannot access your financial data.

Our AI-powered KYC (Know Your Customer) verification process takes under 60 seconds. Simply take a photo of a government-issued ID (passport, driver's license, or national ID card) and a quick selfie. Our system verifies your identity in real time. For business accounts, we may require additional documentation such as a certificate of incorporation, which can be uploaded directly in the app.
