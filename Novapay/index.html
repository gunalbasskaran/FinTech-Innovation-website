The Problem

Legacy Payments Are Broken

Billions of people and businesses face outdated financial infrastructure that was never designed for a digital-first world.

01

Slow Cross-Border Transfers

Traditional wire transfers take 3–5 business days and involve multiple intermediary banks, each adding delays and hidden charges.

02

Hidden Fees Everywhere

Currency conversion markups, processing fees, monthly charges — the average consumer loses 6–8% on every international transaction.

03

Fragmented Experiences

Juggling multiple banking apps, payment wallets, and card networks creates friction. There is no single platform that does it all.

04

Financial Exclusion

Over 1.4 billion adults worldwide remain unbanked, locked out of the global economy due to documentation barriers and infrastructure gaps.
