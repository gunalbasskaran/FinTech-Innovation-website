Solutions Built for Climate Action

From early warning AI to post-disaster recovery — explore the tools that protect communities worldwide.

Prediction Engine

AI-Powered Early Warnings

Our machine-learning models ingest satellite imagery, weather station feeds, ocean buoy data, and historical event patterns to detect disaster precursors 48-72 hours before impact.

  • Multi-model ensemble for 94% prediction accuracy
  • Covers floods, wildfires, hurricanes, heatwaves & earthquakes
  • Continuous learning from new event data worldwide
  • REST API for integration with national meteorological agencies
Geospatial Intelligence

Interactive Risk Mapping

GIS-powered maps overlay flood plains, wildfire fuel loads, seismic fault lines, and population density to reveal exactly where vulnerabilities exist — down to individual neighborhoods.

  • 10-meter resolution satellite base layers
  • Custom risk scoring per zone with exportable reports
  • Historical event overlays for trend analysis
  • Real-time sensor integration for live hazard tracking
Mass Notification

Community Alert System

When seconds count, ResiliNet delivers multi-channel warnings to every resident in an affected zone — simultaneously through SMS, push notifications, email, and integrated sirens.

  • Sub-3-second delivery to 500K+ recipients
  • Geofenced targeting — only affected areas notified
  • Multi-language support with auto-translation
  • Two-way citizen reporting for ground truth verification
Post-Event Operations

Disaster Recovery Hub

From damage assessment to rebuilding, the Recovery Hub coordinates aid distribution, tracks reconstruction progress, and ensures communities return to normalcy faster.

  • AI-powered satellite damage classification
  • Resource allocation engine for aid logistics
  • Citizen-reported needs matching with NGO supply
  • Rebuilding progress dashboard with milestone tracking
